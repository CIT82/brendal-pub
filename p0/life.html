Lily's Life Backstory

Soon family troubles started between mother and father until fathers untimely death 3 months ago. Since then mother Linda played her role as a grieving wife and now widowed and single mother struggling with daily activities. But according to the info I've found on Lily, Linda has already found a new man to call hers and Lily is struggling to love and trust her mother now. She is remembered as a kind and caring girl with many friends out there looking for her and hopefully she will be found soon.
