How to make my famous SPICY! potatoes step by step!

  1. wash and dice 2 potatoes into cubes
  2. dice up 1 sausage
  3. season with oil, salt, paparika, cayanne, ghost pepper
  4. bake in oven for 20 mins
  5. stirr or flip and bake for another 20 mins
  6. serve with chedder and mozzeralla on top and enjoy!
